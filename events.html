Pets in the Park

The Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce is proud to present the 3rd Annual Pets in the Park on Saturday, October 7, 2023, from 10am-2pm at Carolina Beach Lake Park. This event celebrates pets, their owners, and pet-centric businesses in Dog-Friendly Carolina Beach.

The program will include a Pet Blessing, Dog & Cat Adoption Zone, Pet Costume and Pet Trick contests, "Ask the Vet" booth, puppy kissing booth, dog yoga, pet-centric merchandise and service booths, food trucks, DJ, pet portraits, free face painting, and a K9 Demo. There will be a beer & wine garden. *Please note: A valid ID is required and will be checked. Must be 21 or over to enjoy adult libations.

Admission is $5 per person and kids under 10 are free. A portion of the admission price will be donated to participating organizations after the event. All well-behaved pets on leash are welcome. Swag bags will be handed out while supplies last.
